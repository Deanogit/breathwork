What is Breathwork?

Breathwork is a term for various breathing practices in which the conscious control of breathing is said to influence a person's mental, emotional or physical state, with a therapeutic effect.

Breathwork may be helpful for relaxation and stress in a similar way to meditation.

Further research is needed to prove claims that breathwork may be beneficial in improving stress and mental health.
